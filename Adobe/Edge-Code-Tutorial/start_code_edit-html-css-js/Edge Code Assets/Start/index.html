Bromiliads

Colorful tropical plants that can live both indoors and outdoors. They need little water or special care. They come in various different colors, including red, pink, orange and yellow.

Air plants

An air plant is perhaps the easiest plant to take care of, even if you don’t have a green thumb. They need very little water, hence their name, as they seem to live on air only.

Succulents

These hearty plants often grow in deserts or dry climates. For this reason, they store water in their leaves, stems and roots.

More succulents

Because they store so much water, succulents often have a swollen, fleshy appearance. Contrary to common belief, cacti are not succulents.

Ferns

A flowerless plant that has feathery or leafy fronds and reproduces by spores released from the undersides of the fronds.

Butterflies

Though they don’t do the job as much as bees do, butterflies are important pollinators for your plants. And a colorful visitor as well!
